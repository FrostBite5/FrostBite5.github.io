Error Expansion

Error Expansion is a duplicative production boost. Depending on how many pieces you have, you gain a production boost.
Whenever you gain a 1/1 piece size, you can reset your Expansion to start a new one and gain a large production boost.
When you reach certain amounts of Expansions, you can buy upgrades to make getting Expansions easier and buffing them.
