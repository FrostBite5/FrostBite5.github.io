How to play CEG

Computers

Computers aren't a hard concept to grasp. Each computer produces the next power of 10 up. That would mean TI produces 1, TII produces 10, and so on. The errors earned from these computers can be use to buy more computers, higher tier computers, or upgrades.

Production Boost

Production boosts do exactly as they say. They are unlocked at your first TIII computer, and can be bought at 1e3 errors. It gives a 2x boost, unless you have a network (N1=2.5x, N2=3x, N3=3.5x, so on).

Upgrade Computers

Upgrade Computers is the first layer of pretige meant to make the game easier to progress. The first UC costs 10 TI computers, and the second costs 10 TII computers, and so on. Upgrade Computers gives a bonus of about 2x to the tier of computer you bought it with.

I.P. Change

I.P. Change is the second layer of prestige in this game. It is one of the most crucial prestiges in the game. I.P. Change costs 20 of your highest tier computers, and can only be bought when you have maxed UCs (instead of a UC button, it will say "Maxed out!"). This gains the next tier of computer. IP1 gives a Tier V computer, IP2 gives a Tier VI computer, and so on.

Internet Boosts

Internet Boosts replaces I.P. Changes after the fifth one. They can be bought for 35 TIX computers for the first one. It gives about a 2x boost to all computers.

Networks

Networks are the third main prestige. They are the most critical prestiges, as it gives a fairly large boost to production boost. When v1 is released, it will give a 2.5x multiplier on N1, a 3x on N2, and so on. If you are playing the main branch upon time of writing (v0.5), it will give a .01x boost to Production Boost. The first one costs 80 TIX computers.

Downtime

Downtime Challenges (DCs)

Downtime Challenges are the Console Error Game you know, but harder and with special requirements. These are unlocked pre-warning and post-warning, and are very rewarding. Each Downtime Challenge you complete unlocks two upgrades which are known as Downtime Upgrades. Post-warning Downtime Challenges are much harder versions of pre-warning Downtime Challneges and have better rewards.

Downtime Upgrades (DUs)

Downtime Upgrades are rewarded when you complete a downtime challenge. Each DC you complete gives 2 new DUs. (DC1 gives DU1 & 2, DC2 gives DU3 & 4, so on). Post-Warning DUs are stronger versions of Pre-Warning DUs.

Warning

Warning happens when you reach 1.8e308 (or 18^308, as it is also commonly known as.), and gives a warning which can be used for many upgrades. Warning also unlocks a new set of Downtime Challenges and Upgrades.
